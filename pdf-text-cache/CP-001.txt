1. Overview
Introduction and Purpose: This Canada Accessibility Policy ("Policy") outlines Afterpay Canada
Limited ("Afterpay Canada") approach to providing an inclusive and accessible environment for all
Staff Members. This Policy outlines Afterpay Canada's commitment to ensuring accessibility in
accordance with federal and provincial legislation in Canada. The Head of Compliance, Afterpay
Canada is responsible for overseeing this Policy.
Afterpay Canada conducts business in compliance with all applicable laws and regulations Please
refer to the Section: Related Documentation or References of this Policy). The purpose of this
Policy is to further Block's accessibility practices process by ensuring that Afterpay Canada
provides an inclusive and accessible environment for all Staff Members. This Policy outlines
Afterpay Canada's commitment to identifying, removing, and preventing barriers to accessibility in
accordance with federal and provincial legislation in Canada.
Scope: This Policy is established by Afterpay Canada and is applicable to all Afterpay Canada
business lines, and jurisdictions within Canada. It outlines the standards and practices to ensure
accessibility for all Staff Members.
Specific Exclusions: This Policy does not apply to products or services distributed by third parties,
external bank partners, or any subsidiaries operating outside of Canada.
Definitions:
Terminology Definition
Staff Member(s) References to "Staff Member(s)" throughout this document shall refer to
all full-time employees and fixed-term and contingent workers including
interns, temporary workers, consultants, vendors, third-party providers,
and contractors.

2. Policy Requirements
2.1 Commitment to Accessibility
Afterpay Canada will perform the following on a continual basis:
1. Identifying and Removing Barriers: Identifying barriers to accessibility and implementing
measures to remove these barriers;
2. Preventing Barriers: Proactively preventing the creation of new barriers to accessibility;
3. Inclusive Environment: Ensuring Afterpay Canada's services, communications, and
customer interactions are inclusive and accessible to all.
2.2 Federal Legislation
Accessible Canada Act, SC 2019, c 10
The Accessible Canada Act (“ACAˮ) is a federal law that aims to make Canada barrier-free by
2040. It requires organizations under federal jurisdiction to identify, remove, and prevent barriers
in various areas, including information and communication technologies, the built environment, and
the delivery of programs and services.
2.3 Provincial and Territorial Regulations
Ontario; Accessibility for Ontarians with Disabilities Act, 2005 (“AODAˮ)
This Act aims to improve accessibility across Ontario. Afterpay Canada will ensure compliance with
the following:
● Accessible Feedback: Feedback processes must be accessible to persons with disabilities.
Afterpay Canada will provide accessible formats and communication support upon request.
Manitoba; The Accessibility for Manitobans Act, SM 2013, c 40 (“AMAˮ)
This Act aims to eliminate barriers and improve accessibility in Manitoba. Afterpay Canada's
compliance includes:
● Accessible Feedback: Feedback processes will be accessible, with public notifications
about these options provided on Afterpay Canada's website and feedback forms.
● Accessible Communication Formats and Support: Afterpay Canada will provide accessible
formats and communication supports promptly and without extra cost, after consulting with
the requester.

● Customer Service Standard: Implementing policies will ensure the dignity, independence,
and integration of persons with disabilities in Afterpay Canada's service delivery, providing
equal opportunities for access.
Nova Scotia; Accessibility Act, 2017, SNS 2017, c 2
This Act aims to make Nova Scotia accessible by 2030. Afterpay Canada will comply by providing:
● Accessible Feedback: Ensuring feedback processes are accessible, with public
notifications about these options provided on Afterpay Canada's website and feedback
forms.
● Accessible Communication Formats and Support: Providing or arranging for accessible
formats and communication supports promptly and at no extra cost, after consulting with
the individual.
● Customer Service Standard: Developing policies that respect the dignity, independence,
and integration of persons with disabilities, ensuring equal access to services.
Quebec; Act to Secure Handicapped Persons in the Exercise of their Rights with a View to
Achieving Social, School, and Workplace Integration, CQLR c E20.1
This Act promotes the integration of persons with disabilities. Afterpay Canada will ensure
compliance by providing:
● Accessible Feedback: Making feedback processes accessible and notifying the public
about these options on Afterpay Canada's website and feedback forms.
● Accessible Communication Formats and Support: Providing accessible formats and
communication supports promptly and without extra cost, after consulting with the
requester.
● Customer Service Standard: Developing and implementing policies that respect the dignity,
independence, and integration of persons with disabilities, ensuring equal access to
services.
British Columbia; Accessible British Columbia Act, SBC 2021, c 19
This Act aims to improve accessibility in British Columbia. Afterpay Canada will comply by:
● Accessible Feedback: Ensuring feedback processes are accessible, with public
notifications about these options provided on Afterpay Canada's website and feedback
forms.
● Accessible Communication Formats and Support: Providing or arranging for accessible
formats and communication supports promptly and at no extra cost, after consulting with
the individual.
● Customer Service Standard: Developing and implementing policies that respect the dignity,
independence, and integration of persons with disabilities, ensuring equal access to
services.

2.4 Web Accessibility
Afterpay Canada is required to ensure its public websites and web content conforms with the Web
Content Accessibility Guidelines (“WCAGˮ). This includes:
● Conformance Requirements: Ensuring websites and web content meet WCAG 2.0 Level
AA standards, except for criteria requiring live captions and pre-recorded audio
descriptions.
● Exemptions: This requirement does not apply to intranet websites, controlled extensions,
content not directly controlled by Afterpay Canada, or situations where compliance is
impracticable.
2.5 Implementation and Training
Where applicable, Afterpay Canada Compliance will:
1. Training: Provide targeted accessibility training to relevant Staff Members on accessibility
standards that are required and applicable to the respective business areas.
2. Feedback Mechanism: Maintain an accessible feedback process and regularly review
feedback to identify areas for improvement.
3. Regular Audits: Support regular audits of Afterpay Canada's services, websites, and
communication methods to ensure ongoing compliance with accessibility standards.
4. Public Notification: Support internal teams required to notify the public about the
availability of accessible formats and supports through Afterpay Canada's website and
customer service channels.
2.6 Conclusion
Afterpay Canada is committed to creating an inclusive and accessible environment in compliance
with applicable Canadian federal and provincial regulations. This Policy will be regularly reviewed
and updated to ensure ongoing compliance and improvement in accessibility standards. For more
information or to request accessible formats, please visit the "Accessibility" section on Afterpay
Canada's website or contact the customer service team.

3.4 Exceptions
The Policy Owner or designee will have authority to approve exceptions and/or exemptions to this
Policy provided that the proposed exception or exemption does not violate applicable laws and
regulations. Policy exception requests must be documented in writing, include a reason for a

retained with recordkeeping requirements. Any approved exceptions or exemptions should be
submitted to the Policy Governance Team. The Policy Owner and/or designee will inform
Compliance Leadership and applicable committees, as needed, of exceptions.
