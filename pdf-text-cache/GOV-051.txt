1. Introduction
The Compliance Training Calendar (the “Calendar”) is updated and approved on an annual cadence
and outlines the general and regional-specific training schedule for enterprise-wide mandatory
compliance training, delivered through Block’s Learning Management System (“LMS”). This Calendar
is created and maintained in accordance with the Compliance Training Program Standard and is
owned by the Compliance Training Team (“CTT”).
2. Training Calendar
This Compliance Training Calendar is subject to change and any such changes would be subject to
the approval standards of this document outlined in Section 4.2.

5. Exceptions
The Document Owner or designee will have authority to approve exceptions and/or exemptions to
these Standards, provided that the proposed exception or exemption does not violate applicable law.
Document exception requests must be documented in writing, include a reason for a request, and
provide a description of the controls that the Company will implement to mitigate risks associated
with the exception. Any approved exceptions must be documented in writing and retained with
recordkeeping requirements. Any approved exceptions or exemptions should be submitted to the
Policy Governance Team at go/policyhelp. The Document Owner and/or designee will inform
Compliance Leadership and applicable committees, as needed, of exceptions.
