## Purpose

This Code of Conduct establishes the ethical standards and behavioral expectations for everyone acting on behalf of Block Inc.

## Scope

Applies to all Block Inc. employees, officers, directors, contractors, and agents worldwide.

## Policy Statement

Block Inc. is committed to conducting business with integrity, honesty, and respect for all people. All covered individuals must:

- Act honestly and with integrity in all dealings
- Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts promptly
- Protect confidential and proprietary information
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations
- Treat colleagues, customers, and partners with respect and dignity
- Never engage in bribery, corruption, or improper payments
- Report suspected violations without fear of retaliation

## Roles and Responsibilities

**Board of Directors:** Approves and oversees adherence to this Code.

**General Counsel / Chief Compliance Officer:** Maintains the Code and manages the ethics program.

**All Employees:** Read, understand, and comply with this Code; report suspected violations.

**Managers:** Model ethical behavior; create an environment where concerns can be raised safely.

## Compliance and Enforcement

Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination and legal referral. Retaliation against anyone who reports a good-faith concern is strictly prohibited.

Reports can be made to Legal, HR, or anonymously via the ethics hotline.
